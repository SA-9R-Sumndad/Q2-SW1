Role Purpose Meta Heroes
Junglers can be a mage, marksman, assassin, tank, fighter, and even a support hero. They farm creeps for gold in order to buy items quickly. They try to land some kills at the start of the match or "early game" to pressure the enemy. Ling, Julian, Alpha, Yin, Fanny, Martis, and Suyou
Mid Laners are usually mages. They deal high magical damage using their skils in the "early game" up to the "late game". Most mages can one-shot squishy heroes. Zhuxin, Valir, Novaria, Luo Yi, Xavier, Vexana, Kagura
EXP Laners can be a fighter or tank hero. They have durability and life-steal but also deal very high damage. Gatotkaca, Uranus, Chou, Freya, Yu Zhong, Yin, and Ruby
Gold Laners can be a marksman or assassin hero.They deal high damage from a distance using their basic attack. Even though they are squishy heroes, they have high life-steal and critical damage against enemies. They are usually the most powerful in the "late game" Hanabi, Miya, Moskov, Natalia, Melissa, Granger, and Lesley
Roamers can be a tank, assassin, fighter, or support hero. They provide vision and intel on the enemy's whereabouts. They aid their allies and keep an eye on the enemy.They are usually durable but some can be squishy heroes. Estes, Tigreal, Atlas, Hylos, Gloo, Lolita, and Angela
